### Long description

OneAce brings together the simplicity of Sortly and the operational depth of inFlow — without the price tags.

**Why we built it:**
Most inventory software is built for accountants. OneAce is built for the people who actually move stock: warehouse staff, receiving teams, and operations managers who need speed, accuracy, and offline reliability.

**What makes it different:**

• **Offline-first stock counts** — Multiple operators can count simultaneously. Conflicts are detected and surfaced for manual resolution. Everything syncs when you reconnect.

• **Barcode scanning that works** — Continuous scan mode, audio feedback, and unknown-item quick-add. Scan 30 items in 90 seconds — on your phone, no special hardware required.

• **Bin-level inventory tracking** — Assign stock to specific shelves, racks, or zones within each warehouse. Guided putaway after receiving.

• **Purchase orders + scan-assisted receiving** — Scan incoming items against a PO to auto-fill quantities. Post the receipt in one click.

• **Multi-warehouse transfer wizard** — Move stock between locations with a 3-step guided wizard. Scan items to add lines instantly.

**Pricing:**
- Free: 100 items, 1 location, 3 members
- Pro: $29/month — unlimited items and locations, bins, POs, exports
- Business: $79/month — unlimited everything + audit log

Sortly charges $149/month for just 2,000 items with no offline support and no bin tracking. OneAce Pro does everything Sortly Ultra does — and more — for $29.
